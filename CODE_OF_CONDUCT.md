# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience
for everyone, regardless of background or identity.

## Expected behavior

Be respectful, give actionable technical feedback, assume good intent, and
focus disagreement on the work. Harassment, discrimination, threats, and
publication of private information are unacceptable.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately through the repository owner's GitHub
profile. Maintainers may edit, remove, or reject contributions and may suspend
participation when necessary to protect the community.
